- Course focused on "Good Old Fashioned AI" (GOFAI).
	1. Viewing intelligent systems as Agents in the world.
	2. Representing the world, states, actions, etc.
	3. Solving problems by searching.
		- Constraints, uninformed search, heuristic search, game search.
	4. Solving problems by logic and reasoning.
		- Predicate logic, first order logic, inference, and planning.
	5. More complex problems: uncertainty.
		- Reasoning under uncertainty, Bayesian inference, Hidden Markov models.
	6. Introduction to ML, Reinforcement Learning.
